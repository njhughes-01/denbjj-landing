## Outreach Strategy

**For City Rec Centers:**
- Contact main PROS department first for rental process
- Emphasize community benefit, youth programs, affordable pricing
- Propose trial period (3-6 months)

**For CrossFit/Private Gyms:**
- Offer revenue share or flat rental fee
- Emphasize non-competing time slots
- Propose adding value to their existing membership offerings

**For Martial Arts Schools:**
- Position as complementary programming (different focus than traditional martial arts)
- Offer cross-promotion opportunities
- Consider instructor partnership vs pure rental
